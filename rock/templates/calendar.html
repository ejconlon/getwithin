Take this month's health challenge, "Be Physically Active Daily," and get up and get moving today!

"Sun Safety Month"

August

"Know Your Numbers Month"

Do you know whether or not you are at risk for diabetes, heart disease and other chronic conditions? There is a system of “numbers” that allows you to assess where you are in comparison to recommended levels. By knowing your numbers, you can take action to make positive changes that will help prevent the onset of chronic health conditions. Take action to know your numbers by participating in this month's health challenge, "Get Preventive Exams!"
