How does it work?

People meet, are assigned personal coaches that help guide them to achieve wellness goals within the designated areas of Nutrition, Physical Wellness, and Spiritual Balance.

They self-select from a cornucopia of local services, classes, workshops, treatments, events they want to attend and experience the magic of transformation together.

They form support groups and activity buddies along the way to better living.

Your personal Coach will help you outline your goals in the different fields of health creation.

They will begin with starting a health journal with your nutritional goals.
